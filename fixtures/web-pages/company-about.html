About Acme Corp

Building the future of industrial automation since 1985.

Who We Are

Acme Corp is a global leader in industrial widgets and automation solutions, headquartered in Springfield, Illinois. Founded in 1985 by John Q. Founder, we've grown from a small workshop to a company serving over 2,500 clients across 40 countries.

Our mission is to empower manufacturers with reliable, innovative tools that streamline operations and drive growth. We specialize in precision-engineered components, automated assembly systems, and enterprise workflow software.

By the Numbers

  • 350+ employees worldwide
  • 2,500+ active clients
  • 40 countries served
  • $120M annual revenue (2025)
  • 15 patents held
  • 3 R&D centers (Springfield IL, Austin TX, Munich DE)
