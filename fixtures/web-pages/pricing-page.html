Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new rate takes effect immediately and you'll receive a prorated credit for the remainder of your current billing period. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens after the free trial?

After your 14-day trial ends, you'll be prompted to enter payment information. If you choose not to continue, your account will be downgraded to a read-only state. Your data is retained for 30 days, giving you time to export or reactivate.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

Yes. Registered nonprofit organizations receive a 30% discount on all plans. Contact our sales team with your organization's documentation to get started.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), ACH bank transfers, and wire transfers for annual Enterprise contracts.
